Research Archive Matcher (RAM) is a secure, offline-first 
research document intelligence platform. It allows you to:

  - Scan and recursively index directories of PDF papers.
  - Automatically identify structural document types (Articles, Books, TOCs).
  - Detect exact hash duplicates and potential title duplicates in your library.
  - Instantly search your index offline using full-text or field queries.
  - Align external citation lists (Excel, Word, TXT) with local PDFs.
  - Generate customized Excel matrices, Word executive summaries, and 
    interactive HTML browser dashboards.

All parsing, NLP similarity, and matching computation are executed 
100% locally on your computer, ensuring absolute privacy for your 
research data.
